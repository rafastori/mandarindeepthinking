A: Xiao Wang, welcome to our company. You have read the contract, so do you have any questions?
B: I would like to ask how are insurances at our company paid for?
A: The state regulation of "Five social insurances and one housing fund".
B: Sorry, I just returned to China. I don't quite understand.
A: "Five social insurances and one housing fund" is: old age insurance, medical insurance, unemployment insurance, work disability insurance, maternity insurance and housing accumulation fund.
B: Does the company pay for all of these?
A: Old age insurance, medical insurance, unemployment insurance and the housing accumulation fund are paid by the company and the individual together.
B: Then how much needs to be paid?
A: Every city is different. In Shanghai, for the old age insurance the individual pays 8\\%, and work pays 21\\%. For medical insurance the individual pays 2\\% and work pays 11\\%. For unemployment insurance the individual pays 0.5\\% and work pays 1.5\\%. The individual and work each pay 7\\% for the accumulation fund.
B: Then, my residence permit is not in Shanghai, so do I have to pay according to Shanghai's criterion?
A: Yes, because you work in Shanghai.
B: If I retire, can I collect old age insurance from Shanghai?
A: This is rather a nuisance. If you retire, but your residence permit is not in Shanghai, then you can only go to the place of your permanent residence to collect old age insurance.
B: It's so complicated.
