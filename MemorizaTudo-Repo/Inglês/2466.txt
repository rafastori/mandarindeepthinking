A: Lili, after work can you go with me to the shopping mall to walk around?
B: Sure. What do you want to buy?
A: My close friend is getting married. I would like to pick out a wedding gift.
B: Wouldn't it be fine to just give her a red envelope with money in it?
A: She is my best friend. I want to give her a gift that is different.
B: Have you thought of what you would like to buy?
A: Not yet. Do you have any good ideas?
B: It's quite common to give things like tableware as a wedding gift.
A: That's too ordinary. I want something a bit more special.
B: Then get something traditional. How about a lacquer comb?
A: Oh? What meaning is attached to it?
B: In China, this signifies that two people will always be together.
A: That's great. Let's go take a look.
