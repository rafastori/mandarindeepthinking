A: Hotels are so expensive!
B: Do you want to go traveling?
A: Yeah. I'm planning to travel to New York for fun over the summer. But how can the hotels be so expensive?
B: Maybe you should look on Airbnb?
A: What's that?
B: It's an internet site that lets you book rooms. However, they aren't hotels, they're just rooms in people's homes. The landlord rents out his own spare rooms and makes a bit of money.
A: I see. Hey, there are a lot! This looks pretty good. A bit more than 600 RMB per night. It also has a kitchen, so I can cook for myself.
B: However, this isn't an apartment, it's just a room. You have to share the kitchen and the bathroom with others.
A: Oh, that doesn't matter. But, is staying with other people safe?
B: You can look at the reviews. They're written by people who stayed there before. If a lot of people have commented, and the ratings are high it shouldn't be a problem.
A: That's great. I'll keep looking!
